## Overview of the Project
The purpose of this project is to identify the difference between types of algorithms strategies to solve the knapsack optimization problem. This experiment is used to justify what is the best way algorithmically to fill a "bag" and produce the most efficent Rating and Weight ratio without exceeding a weight limit boundary.

In our project we will focuse on a handful of different algorithms that are not to exceed the total weight of 700kg.

## The Implemented Operations
**Part 1 - Implement Three Greedy Strategies**

#### Requirements:
* Highest Rating First.  
* Lightest Weight First.  
* Best Rating-to-Weight Ratio First.  
<br>

**Part 2 - Brute Force / Exhaustive Search**

#### Requirements:
* Generates 4096 Possible Subsets.  
* Computes Total Weight and Total Rating.  
* Displays Three Highest-Rated Subsets and Indicates Most Optimal Subset. 
<br>

**Part 3 - Comparison Summary Output**

#### Requirements:
* Results From Part 1.  
* Results from Part 2.  
* Summary Comparing Part 1 and Part 2 For Which Strategies Align and Which Don't.  
<br>

**Part 4 - Dynamic Programming**

#### Requirements:
* Psuedocode Comments Explaining How This Solution Works.<br>
  #### OR <br>
* Implementation Of Dynamic Programming Code.  
<br>
